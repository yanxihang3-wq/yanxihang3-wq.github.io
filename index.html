About Us

Cixi Feihang Sanitary Ware Co., Ltd. is a leading manufacturer dedicated to the production of high-quality bathroom fixtures. We specialize in engineering durable and stylish shower heads, bidet sprays, and essential bathroom accessories for modern homes and commercial spaces.
